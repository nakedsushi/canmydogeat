Can My Dog Eat _______

Messenger chat bot that answers your "Can my dog eat _____" questions.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Dogs eat everything, but should they?

Dogs don't have the most discerning appetites but there are so many things that they shouldn't eat. It's hard to keep track of what's going to make your dog sick. We keep track of it so you don't have to.

Usage

To ask if your dog can eat something, just ask our chat bot, "Can my dog eat ______?"
